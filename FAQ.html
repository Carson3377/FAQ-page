Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Why join our team in NavBar cafe?

As you know that NavBar cafe is one of the most popular cafe in the town where most developers chill with coffee break and enjoy listening music while coding. A soothing place for everyone to enjoy and employees get to enjoy too! You'll get a free choice of coffee if you start your first day job. Welcome to NavBar cafe!

Do you offer a barista training course in the cafe?

Yes, we do offer barista training at the same time work. Don't worry if you possess no experience as long as you got the passion in coffee art making and we also hire bartender. Needless to say, must be 18 yo and above...

What's the salary and how long for part-time and full-time job?

For part-timer, your basic salary per hour will be $7-$10. As for full-timer will be $1500-$3000 per month. The working hour for part-timer is 6-8 hours and 15 hours for full-timer. For your information, our business hour begins from 9am - 1am midnight daily (Mon-Sun). Customers stay as long as they want until 1am.

What foods does the cafe serve?

Well, if you're a fan of western foods, desserts, seafoods. You've come to the right place! We serve western foods and even seafoods here, and also wines at the bar counter. However, wines are only available from 5pm onwards.
